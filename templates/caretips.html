Brahmi

Brahmi is a medicinal herb known for enhancing memory and reducing stress. It thrives in moist, warm environments. Keep the soil consistently damp but not waterlogged, and place it in partial sunlight. Regular pruning encourages bushy growth and prevents legginess.

Haworthia

Haworthia is a small, hardy succulent ideal for indoor spaces. It prefers bright, indirect sunlight and well-draining soil. Water sparinglyâ€”only when the soil is completely dry. Avoid overwatering, as it can lead to root rot. Minimal care makes it great for beginners.

Jade

The Jade plant is a symbol of good luck and prosperity. It loves bright light and can even tolerate some direct sunlight. Water deeply but infrequently, allowing the soil to dry out between watering. Prune to maintain shape and encourage branching.

Bird of Paradise

This striking tropical plant loves bright, direct sunlight and regular watering. Keep the soil moist during the growing season and reduce watering in winter. Its large leaves benefit from occasional misting to boost humidity. Fertilize monthly in spring and summer.

Golden Thuja

Golden Thuja is a conifer with beautiful yellow foliage. It thrives in full sun and requires well-drained soil. Water regularly when young, but once established, it becomes drought-tolerant. Ideal for hedging and ornamental landscaping, prune it lightly to shape.

Verbena

Verbena is a colorful flowering plant that thrives in full sun. It requires moderately fertile soil and consistent watering. Deadhead spent blooms to promote continuous flowering. Ideal for hanging baskets, borders, and attracting pollinators like butterflies and bees.
