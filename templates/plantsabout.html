🌿 About Nature’s Basket

Nature’s Basket is a simple yet powerful web-based platform designed to make plant ownership easier and more enjoyable. It helps users explore a wide variety of plants, purchase them online, and get proper care instructions to keep them healthy.

🌱 Our Vision

To promote a greener environment by making plant care and inventory management accessible to everyone — from beginners to plant enthusiasts.

💡 Key Features

  • 🪴 Online Plant Store – Browse and buy from a wide collection of indoor and outdoor plants.
  • 📦 Inventory Management – Keeps track of available plants and their details like category, price, and quantity.
  • 📋 Customer Registration & Login – Secure and simple user authentication system.
  • 🧼 Care Instructions – Offers tailored care tips for each plant.
  • 📸 Mini Blog & Updates – Users can share their plant care experiences and stories.

🛠 Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python with Flask Framework
  • Database: MySQL
  • Tools: VS Code, MySQL

👩‍💻 Developer Note

This project was developed as part of a final-year BCA project to demonstrate real-world application design using web technologies and databases. It combines creative UI with a functional backend system.
